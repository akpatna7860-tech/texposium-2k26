ABOUT SCE

Supaul College of Engineering, is located in Bihar. Established in 2017, it is a public institute approved by AICTE. Supaul College of Engineering provides 7 UG and PG courses in 6 specialisations, namely Civil Engineering, Computer Science Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. Theses programmes are offered in Full Time mode. B.E. / B.Tech and M.E./M.Tech are some of the courses offered at Supaul College of Engineering. ABOUT TECHXPOSIUM

TechXposium is the flagship technical fest of SCE Supaul, organized by the Technical Club under the guidance of faculty advisors. The fest brings together students, professionals and innovators to celebrate technology, learning and collaboration.
